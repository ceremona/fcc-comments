Topics What topics are within comments? We're able to create a hierarchical structure of the most common topics within commenters' messages. To explore topics, use the large sunburst. Hover to display topics, click to zoom in, and click the center to zoom out. The smaller sunburst will help give you some perspective of the overall topic structure. This analysis was run on 447,000 FCC comments. Learn more about the science behind the visualization here.
